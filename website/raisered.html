RaiseRED

RaiseRED is a year-long fundraiser that fights to end pediatric cancer and blood disease. Each February, it concludes with an 18 hour dance marathon. All of the proceeds go to the University of Louisville pediatric hematology and oncology program. Half of the money raised goes toward the research of pediatric hematology and oncology. The other half goes directly to the families of children in the program to help them pay with the burdensome expenses.
I got involved with RaiseRED as a freshman by raising money and dancing at the marathon with my fraternity, Sigma Phi Epsilon.
